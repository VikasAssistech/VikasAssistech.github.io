Pursuits of Life:

Life is such an incredible journey which should be lived with patience and believe to pursue four major objectives.

  • DHARMA: The quality of being morally right or justifiable. DHARMA is a comprehensive, it covers an entire range of values.
  • ARTHA: Acquisition of wealth by proper means and its right use.
  • KAMA: Fulfillment of noble desires.
  • MOKSHA: Liberation or the final Goal.(It requires arduous spiritual practice and usually takes efforts on many births)
It is essential to have the proper understanding of these objectives (as also because these terms are highly used in our daily language to mean different things)
