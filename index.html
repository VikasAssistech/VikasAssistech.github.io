About me

Hi, I am a Research Scholar in assistive technology lab, supervised by Prof. M. Balakrishnan, Dr. Rohan Paul and Prof. P.V.M. Rao. I am also part of project IncluNav, Assistech Lab. I have done my Bachelor and Master of Technology from University of Allahabad, India. Earlier, I have worked with National Institute of Electronics. Before joining for my PhD in Khoshla School of Information Technology, IIT Delhi, I was an associate researcher in Assistech Lab, Department of Computer Science in IIT Delhi, India. My research at IIT Delhi touches upon indoor navigation and accessibility issues to understand and create sustainable technology solution that can analyze an annotate building map and suggest the possible cue manifestation to create an accessible indoor space. I am also interested in developing affordable technology solution for people with visual disabilities and old age. I have some experience in doing so through multiple assistive technological innovations and research projects at Assistech Lab in partnership with disability organizations like NAB and Saksham and other stakeholders like AIIMS and WHO-SEARO.
